Hi! I'm Denis

I'm a minimalist frontend designer & developer. I enjoy the creation of meaningful experiences that digital technologies can reveal.

I live in Barcelona and work have fun at Friend2Friend, where I had the pleasure to collaborate in projects for great clients such as Samsung, Nickelodeon, Universal, Microsoft, Intel, IKEA, Calvin Klein, IE Business School, University of Oxford. In my free time I create experiments. You can download my CV if you wish.

I'm Cuban and love playing my bass guitar.
